Info.plist

AppLovin

Go to Project Settings. Select "Info", right-click on one of the rows of "Custom iOS Properties" and select "Add Row". The key of the new row should be  " AppLovinSdkKey " without quotes and the value: YOUR_APPLOVIN_KEY
